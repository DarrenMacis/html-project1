Who Am I

I am Darren Macis, the dedicated and strategic Director of Operations and Owner at Macis & McRae Services Ltd., a dynamic delivery and courier company based in Elgin, Moray.
With a focus on providing reliable and efficient delivery services, we specialize in distributing newspapers across the Highlands and delivering parcels for InPost to local lockers within commercial locations.
Our operations are robust, extending 7 days a week, 363 days a year, ensuring consistent service to our clients.
Our company is committed to upholding the highest standards of service, facilitated by a fleet of well-maintained vans and a team of professional drivers, each vetted for their expertise and dedication to safety.
Although Macis & McRae Services Ltd. was initially established without a VAT number, we have successfully navigated the complexities of business growth, including VAT registration and compliance, under my guidance.
As the leader of Macis & McRae Services Ltd., I prioritize transparency, efficiency, and innovation. I am currently focused on enhancing our operational frameworks, expanding our service reach, and maintaining compliance with all regulatory requirements.
Our goal is not only to meet the logistical needs of our clients but also to exceed them, ensuring that Macis & McRae Services Ltd. remains synonymous with trust, reliability, and quality in the delivery and courier industry.
